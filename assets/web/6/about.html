About Us

DWI TUNGGAL ELECTRIC

CV Dwi Tunggal Electric is a company that has been established since 2007. We are a company engaged in the field of Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing (MEP) and we are currently expanding also to manufacture panels. Our company is sufficiently developed in Central Java, precisely in the city of Semarang.

Our goal is to provide the best service for customers. We strive for all the jobs on time, competitive prices and more of our priorities, which are satisfactory results / output and according to customer expectations.
